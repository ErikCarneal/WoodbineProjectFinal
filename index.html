History

Woodbine has been a town ever since before the American Civil War. It served as a key spot in the famous Battle of Gettysburg. Woodbine is where the Confederate Army stayed to scout the Union Army. After the Civil War, Woodbine was largely known for the succesful canning factory located here.

Woodbine Now

Today, Woodbine is known for a few main attractions. One of these main attractons is Larriland Farm, which is a famous pick your own fruit farm. Other attractions include Days End Horse Farm and The Woodbine Inn.
